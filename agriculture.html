Agriculture

Poultry Farming

We recognize the immense potential of poultry farming as a sustainable and profitable agricultural venture. Through this initiative, we equip poultry farmers with the necessary skills, knowledge, and resources to thrive in the poultry industry. From understanding proper housing and ventilation to implementing effective feeding strategies and disease prevention measures, participants gain a deep understanding of best practices in poultry farming. The program covers key areas such as breed selection, chick rearing, broiler production, egg production, and poultry health management. With a focus on sustainable and profitable poultry farming, we not only enable farmers to achieve economic self-sufficiency but also promote the overall development and prosperity of the community.

Sweet Potato Farming

Through the sweet potato farming initiative, we equip farmers with the knowledge, resources, and support needed to excel in sweet potato cultivation. Farmers receive comprehensive training on various aspects of sweet potato production. They learn essential techniques such as land preparation, planting, irrigation, pest and disease management, and harvesting. The program emphasizes sustainable agricultural practices, promoting organic farming methods and the efficient use of resources. We provide participants with quality sweet potato planting materials, ensuring the use of improved varieties that exhibit high yields and resilience to local conditions. Furthermore, we actively explore market opportunities for sweet potato farmers, connecting them with potential buyers and promoting value addition. By encouraging farmers to diversify their products and explore innovative uses for sweet potatoes, such as processing into flour or snacks, the organization supports income generation and the development of local value chains.

Groundnut Farming

Farmers in the groundnut farming program receive comprehensive training on various aspects of groundnut production. They learn essential techniques such as land preparation, seed selection, planting, irrigation, pest and disease management, and harvesting. The program emphasizes sustainable agricultural practices, promoting soil conservation, water management, and integrated pest management. We explore market opportunities for groundnut farmers, connecting them with potential buyers and promoting value addition. We also explore innovative uses for groundnuts, such as processing into oil, butter, or snacks, to support income generation and the development of local value chains. Through this initiative, we not only promote food security and sustainable agriculture but also enhance the economic well-being of farmers and the community.

Dairy Farming

Farmer receive comprehensive training on essential skills such as animal care, feeding, breeding, milking techniques, and disease prevention. The program emphasizes the importance of animal welfare, proper nutrition, and hygiene practices to ensure high-quality milk production. Moreover, we support dairy farmers by facilitating market linkages and value addition opportunities. We explore avenues for farmers to process and market dairy products such as fresh milk, yogurt, and sour milk. By assisting farmers in accessing markets, we help them increase their income and profitability.
