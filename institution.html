Welcome to Bukura Educational Complex!


Bukura Educational Complex is a reputable educational institution that provides primary school education while offering vital support to less fortunate learners within the community. Our institution follows the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC), a modern and learner-centered approach that focuses on developing essential skills and knowledge. With a commitment to inclusive education, we strive to create an environment where every child has equal opportunities to thrive academically and personally. Through our comprehensive educational programs, we empower learners with the necessary competencies, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity. We believe in the transformative power of education and work diligently to ensure that all learners, regardless of their backgrounds, receive a quality education that equips them for a successful future.
